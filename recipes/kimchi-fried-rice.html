Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice that sends you straight into a small home in Seoul. The combination of kimchi with pork, along with the rice will make your meal statisfactory, whether you are eating by yourself or sharing with others. Feel free to replace the pork with meat of choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tb canola oil
  • 1/2 cup diced pork
  • 1 cup kimchi, drained and chopped
  • 1 Tb soy sauce
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 green onion sliced, with white and green parts separated
  • 1 tb sesame oil
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Heat 1 Tb of canola oil in pan over high heat. Add the pork and the white parts of green onion into the pan. Cook until meat is brown.
  2. Push the meat and green onion to one side of the pan and add the soy sauce to the other side so that the sauce touches nothing but the pan. Once the soy sauce simmers, add the kimchi and mix all the ingredients together.
  3. Once the kimchi juice reduces a bit, add the cooked rice and stir until the rice is fully coated with the sauce.
  4. On a separate pan, heat 1Tb of canola oil over medium high. Crack egg into the pan and cook until yolk is thick. Place egg over fried rice.
